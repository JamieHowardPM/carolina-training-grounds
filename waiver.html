CTG Liability Waiver

Every participant must complete a liability waiver before training at Carolina Training Grounds. This applies to:

  • Students attending CTG classes or events
  • Participants in programs taught by independent instructors
  • Guests attending hosted seminars or special events

The form covers assumption of risk, medical readiness, and consent for emergency care. Parents or legal guardians must complete the minor section for anyone under 18.

You only need to complete the waiver once per person unless your information changes. If you have questions about the waiver or how your information is used, email info@carolinatraininggrounds.com.
