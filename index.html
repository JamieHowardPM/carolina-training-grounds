A space that feels like training, not a conference room.

The facility is built for high movement training and small to mid sized groups. It supports striking based programs, self defense, conditioning, and skills workshops.

  • Padded training floor for drills and partner work
  • Open layout for classes, stations, and small groups
  • Access to striking pads and training equipment as available
  • Clean, focused environment with restroom and parking

The goal is simple: give you a space that feels professional and ready the moment students walk in.

How instructors and groups use Carolina Training Grounds
