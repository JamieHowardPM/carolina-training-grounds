The Facility

Carolina Training Grounds is designed as a focused training environment. The space is set up for striking, self defense, and small group fitness with clear sightlines and minimal distractions.

Dedicated Mat Area

Open floor plan with matted training area suitable for striking, pad work, and partner drills. Layout can be adjusted for seminars or workshops.

Equipment Ready

Storage supports class equipment such as pads, shields, and light fitness tools. Exact equipment available depends on the programs in session.

Community Scale

Built for small to mid sized classes that value coaching, safety, and connection over high traffic gym environments.

Programs & Partners

Carolina Training Grounds is the facility home for selected training programs. Each program manages its own curriculum, memberships, and communications while sharing the same training space.
