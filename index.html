Carolina Training Grounds is a dedicated training facility in Chapel Hill for self defense, striking, and small group fitness. It is built for coaches and programs that need a clean, dependable space to train.

The Facility

The space is set up for striking, pad work, and partner drills with clear sightlines and a calm, focused training environment.

Facility Snapshot

Built Around The Training Floor

Carolina Training Grounds centers everything on the mats. It is designed so classes can move, strike, and drill without fighting for space or working around general gym traffic.

  • Open matted floor for striking and self defense drills
  • Space for small and mid sized groups
  • Room for pads, shields, and light training equipment
  • Layout that supports coaching, demos, and observation

Who Trains At Carolina Training Grounds

The facility is the home base for selected programs that manage their own memberships and curriculum while sharing the same training space.

Primary Program

Krav Maga Chapel Hill

Krav Maga Chapel Hill offers practical self defense and striking based conditioning for adults and teens. Classes focus on real world skills, clear coaching, and a supportive training community.

  • Foundations and Level 1+ Krav Maga
  • Krav Fit style conditioning sessions
  • Training designed for everyday people
