About Carolina Training Grounds

Carolina Training Grounds is a dedicated space for group classes, small-group training, and events. The facility is designed to support striking arts, self defense, fitness programs, and community-focused workshops.

Our goal is to provide a clean, professional space that instructors and organizations can use without the overhead of managing a full-time gym. You bring the program. We provide the floor, equipment, and environment.

The Facility

The space is built to support high-movement training and small to mid-sized groups. It is ideal for striking-based classes, self defense training, functional fitness, and skills workshops.

Training Floor

  • Padded floor for striking and movement drills
  • Open layout for classes and seminars
  • Space for small-group work and stations

Equipment

  • Striking pads and bags (as available)
  • Portable training gear for partner drills
  • Whiteboard or display area for instruction

Environment

  • Clean, focused training atmosphere
  • Restroom access for participants
  • Parking and easy access from Chapel Hill

Host Your Class, Seminar, Or Event

Use Carolina Training Grounds for one-time seminars, recurring classes, or private training blocks. The space can support fitness sessions, self defense workshops, instructor-led programs, and small-group events.
