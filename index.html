About Carolina Training Grounds

Carolina Training Grounds provides a clean, open, and professional environment for instructors, programs, and organizations that need a reliable training space.

Whether you're hosting a class, seminar, fitness session, or private training block, CTG gives you the room, equipment access, and support to run your program smoothly.
