About Carolina Training Grounds

Carolina Training Grounds is a dedicated training facility designed to support instructors, programs, and community groups that need a reliable space for classes and events.

The space works well for striking arts, self defense, conditioning, and workshops. You bring the program. CTG provides the floor, equipment access, and environment.

Facility Features

A clean, open layout built for movement, impact work, and group training.

Training Floor

  • Padded flooring for striking and movement drills
  • Open space for small to mid-sized groups
  • Suitable for classes, seminars, and workshops

Equipment

  • Striking pads and bags (as available)
  • Portable training gear for partner drills
  • Whiteboard or display area for instruction

Environment

  • Clean, focused training atmosphere
  • Restroom access
  • Parking and convenient Chapel Hill access

Programs & Uses

CTG can support ongoing programs, short-term intensives, and one-time events. The layout works well for striking-based classes, strength and conditioning, and skills training.

  • Weekly or monthly fitness or self defense classes
  • Weekend seminars or specialty workshops
  • Clinics, intensives, or focused training blocks
  • Private or small-group training sessions
