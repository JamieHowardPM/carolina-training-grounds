Student Training Sessions

Purchase single sessions or class packs for student training at CTG. Current rates are shown on the secure checkout page.

Single Session 5-Class Pack (5 sessions) 10-Class Pack (10 sessions)

Each pack equals the number of sessions listed. After purchase you will receive a confirmation email. Please arrive a few minutes early, wear athletic clothing, and bring water and (optionally) a small workout towel. For safety and cleanliness, outside shoes are not permitted on the mat.

Teach Your Program at CTG

CTG – Teach (Instructor Facility Use)

For independent instructors running their own classes, workshops, or recurring programs. Instructor rates are arranged directly with CTG and paid through private Stripe links.

  • Suitable for striking, self defense, conditioning, and small group formats
  • You manage your students, we provide the facility and environment
  • Simple structure with clear agreements before any payments are made

Once your program is approved, we will send you a private Stripe link for your reserved hours or blocks so you can handle payments securely.
