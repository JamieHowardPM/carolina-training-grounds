Student Training Sessions

Purchase single sessions or class packs for student training at CTG.

Single Session – $25 5-Class Pack (5 sessions) – $110 10-Class Pack (10 sessions) – $200

Each pack equals the number of sessions listed. After purchase you will receive a confirmation email. Please arrive a few minutes early, wear athletic clothing, and bring water and (optionally) a small workout towel. For safety and cleanliness, outside shoes are not permitted on the mat.

All participants must also complete a liability waiver before training: CTG Liability Waiver

Host an Event, Seminar, or Special Session

CTG – Host (Event / Seminar Rental)

Use the facility for one-time seminars, visiting schools, clinics, or special events. Pricing is structured for straightforward planning.

  • Hourly event rental starting at $65/hour
  • Half-day blocks (up to 3 hours)
  • Full-day or multi-block events available by agreement

Event pricing varies based on duration, equipment use, and expected headcount. We will confirm schedule and provide a payment link once details are approved.

Teach Your Program at CTG

CTG – Teach (Instructor Hourly Facility Use)

For independent instructors running their own classes, workshops, or recurring programs. Instructor rates are hourly and set up individually after approval.

  • Baseline instructor facility rate: $45/hour (subject to agreement)
  • Intended for instructors bringing their own students
  • Simple structure: you handle your students, we provide the floor and environment

At this time, instructor facility use is arranged directly with CTG. Once approved, you will receive a private Stripe link to handle payments for your reserved hours.
